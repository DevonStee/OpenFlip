OpenFlip is a Braun-inspired flip clock for Android, focusing on mechanical animation fidelity, visual precision, and maintainable architecture.

Key features:
- Custom rendering engine: Physics-based flip animations using Canvas + 3D transforms.
- Simulated bulb light: Warm light overlay recreates the glow of a real desk lamp.
- Rotary knob time control: Drag the knob to fast-flip through digits with momentum.
- Hourly chime: Configurable chime sound on the hour.
- Sleep timer: Set a countdown to automatically close the app.
- OLED burn-in protection: Subtle pixel shifting for screen longevity.
- Zero-drain background behavior: Stops all rendering when not visible.
